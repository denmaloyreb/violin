First lesson program

To determine your musical abilities and start training, you need to sign up for the first lesson at the school of music and vocals, where you will have the opportunity to get acquainted with teachers, learn all about the unique accelerated program and much more.

You don't need to pay for it — the first lesson is absolutely free!

The choice of the teacher

Choice of the teacher at the request or on the recommendation of the administrator. The vocal teacher will continue to teach vocals and pave the way for success.

Basic exercise

Performing the first basic exercises under the guidance of a teacher, in order to see the result of singing at the first lesson. In the basic exercises include warm-UPS and training of articulation.

Selection of the training program

And finally, if you want further training, a special accelerated program is selected, suitable separately for everyone who wants to learn vocals from scratch.

The definition of a musical level

This is necessary to determine the future program of adult vocal training. The teacher will ask you to sing a simple song or repeat the melody you heard. This determines the musical ear, voice capabilities and sense of rhythm, as well as the artistry of a novice performer.

Voice processing

Based on the results, an experienced teacher gives a number of tips and recommendations in the field of vocals to a future student of the SKIF school.
